  Important message: Coronavirus

Please see below regarding steps we are taking are asking our patients to take in light of the Coronavirus outbreak:

Your Health and Safety are our priority:

We are incorporating the following into our routine

  • Being extra careful with our hand hygiene and personal protective equipment
  • Reducing the number of staff in direct contact with you
  • Asking our staff to take sick leave even for a common cold

We would appreciate it if you could assist us by

  • Minimising the number of people attending the appointment e.g additional siblings or friends or anyone not required in the surgery area
  • Reducing any unnecessary appointments
  • Rescheduling an appointment when you are UNWELL even if it is just a cold. We will take this into consideration and provide the soonest available appointment once you are well again.

If you have travelled abroad in the last 14 days or been in close contact with someone with confirmed coronavirus please check the gov.uk website for the latest COVID-19 travel advice. You may need to call NHS 111 for further advice before making or attending your appointment.

As a team, we commit to standardise all infection control and personal protective protocols and to avoid cross contamination of any virus, to us and our patients.

Please let us know if you or a family member has travelled abroad in the last 3 weeks.

Free virtual consultation

Send us your photos and we'll get back to you with our assessment. This is completely free and there is no obligation to proceed with treatment. Get in touch today and start your smile journey.

Please ensure that your photos look like the examples to the right so that we can carry out a full examination of your teeth, mouth and smile.

How to take your pictures

Watch the video below to learn how to take clear photos of your teeth from the angles that help us to best assess your smile.

What are the next steps?

  • After you send us your photos and information our treatment coordinator will review everything
  • We will get back to you within 3 business days
  • We will provide you with a general estimate of time and treatment cost based on Dr. Styrt’s initial assessment of the photos you provided
  • To start treatment you would schedule a time to come to our office to take your orthodontic records (digital impressions, x-rays, photographs), have an examination with Dr. Styrt, and finalize your plan!

What will happen at your appointment?

  • We’ll sit down with you to discuss why you want orthodontic treatment and what you hope to get out of it.

  • Our Treatment Coordinator will do an initial examination of your teeth and facial profile, along with taking photos, so we know exactly what level of treatment you’ll benefit from.

  • We’ll explain the treatment procedure that we’d recommend you have and give you a quote.
